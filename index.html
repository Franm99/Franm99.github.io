About me

I'm a junior software developer with a strong interest in learning new things every day. I enjoy challenging myself to improve my knowledge in different software development fields, such as Machine Learning, Data Science and Web development.

During my studies of Electronic Engineering, Robotics and Mechatronics at the University of Malaga(Spain), I gained technical knowledge about the functioning of automatic and industrial systems, both on the hardware and software side, and manipulation and autonomous robotics techniques. Here I realized for the first time how fascinating and challenging the world of software development can be, especially when it is applied to improve people's lives.

Due to the curiosity that began to grow inside me about this field, I applied for studying a Master’s degree in Intelligent Systems at Universitat Jaume I, in Castellón (Spain). Personally, I see the year this studies occupied (2021) as the most valuable for my student career. Here, I keep learning about robotics, and how artificial intelligence can be applied to them for social service purposes. Machine learning was the software field in which I spent most of my time, particularly in my Master’s Thesis.

At this time, I’m working on a research project for an AI-based solution applied to medical image analysis. Although my career has started focusing on Machine Learning development, I’m very open-minded and try to learn from fields that are not really related. One of them is Web development. That’s why I felt the necessity to build up my own portfolio as a personal project.
